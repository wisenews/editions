Edition Arc & Coherence 76

A solid Tergar-Vajrayana edition with a strong opening, a strong closing, and consistent voice work. The compliance level is high (commentaries are within range, dialogues are complete and properly attributed, lenses have concept fields). Two systematic issues drag the arc score: (1) klesha is overweighted to 'wonder' (3 of 5), and (2) the 'transcending' method label is applied to two practices that are actually rejoicing/transforming in content — both Dalai Lama wonder pieces. The penguin story has a story-vs-commentary klesha mismatch (grief at story level, wonder at commentary level) that should be resolved. Voice variety is on the thin side with only Mingyur and Dalai Lama in primary. Sources are real and well-attested but two URLs (LiveScience, CBS News) returned 404 on fetch and should be verified.
